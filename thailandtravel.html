A Year in Thailand

Things that still blow my mind:

I showed up around 1-2 months after receiving the position with very little time to prepare - how did I have the guts to do that??

I knew absolutely 0 people

I knew 0 Thai other than the Youtube videos I tried to watch before showing up

Things I learned:

Conversational Thai (ish)

I simply cannot drive a motorbike ... BUT I am a regular at riding on the back

The difficulty of attaining citizenship status in Thailand + difficulties in living when not a citizen

There are indigenous groups, termed "Hill Tribes" (which in itself is a politicized term), who do not hold citizenship, are stateless, and lack rights despite being native to the land for centuries or longer

There is so much more to Thai food than pad thai
